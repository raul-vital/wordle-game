Wordle Game - Countries Edition

Get 5 chances to guess a 5-letter country.

How to play:

* A green box indicates that you've guessed the correct letter in the correct place in the word.

* A yellow box means you've guessed a letter that's in the word, but not in the right spot.

* A gray box means that letter is not in the word.
